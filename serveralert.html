Formation of Eden


In the year 115 [3A], Thellyn Thelsaron reformed Quel'thalas into Eden, uniting the regions under the names Naru, Fey'el, and Dovaheim. With this reformation, it was decreed that each new emperor would ensure noble leadership passed to the next generation, marking the end of the elven era and the dawn of Thelsaron rule, bringing forth a vision of unity.
